Meet Our Panel of Small Business Experts

Sponsored content in partnership with Mercedes Benz

This year, Fast Company and Mercedes-Benz have invited three small business advocates, experts, and entrepreneurs to provide insights and commentary on the Drive Your Business Forward Reader's Choice Award nominees.

Check back starting July 7th to see each award nominee introduced and to hear what our panel of experts has to say!

  • Jamie Wong

    Co-Founder & CEO, Vayable

    Jamie Wong (@jamiejwong) is the Cofounder and CEO of San Francisco travel startup, Vayable, an online platform building amazing vacations. As an entrepreneur, writer, and producer, Jamie Wong is the true twenty-first century storyteller. Her outstanding ability to generate fresh, creative and thoughtful solutions to day-to-day problems has afforded Jamie the privilege to collaborate with some of the best minds and leaders in technology and media. Jamie’s experience in technology and communications coupled with her vision for greater collaborative exchange in travel is the driving force behind Vayable. Her commitment to bringing insider travel experiences to the world by making them easier to find, create and book provides the framework for Vayable's platform and community. Before co-founding Vayable, she was a creative and strategy consultant for several top tech companies including Kickstarter, Adobe, and Boku.
